About DigiRail

DigiRail is part of DVT

In late 2019 technology entrepreneurs Tim Robinson and Chris Wilkins met to exchange notes on an opportunity to collaborate. Tim, a digital leader with a passion for rail had seen an opportunity emerging to bring fresh thinking around digital transformation to a global industry. Chris, the founder and Chairman of DVT and an experienced builder of global technology businesses was looking to take the groups technology expertise and market leading products to new industries. DigiRail was born.

DigiRail has a single industry focus backed by a multi-national, multi-disciplinary delivery capability.

As a specialist boutique provider we will innovate technologically and commercially but we will deliver with the certainty and scale of a major business.
